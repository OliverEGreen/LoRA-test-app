kodachrome, Downtown Chicago skyline view from a rail yard, late-1930s. Cluster of stone-clad early-skyscrapers in setback Art Deco and Beaux-Arts styles — slender central tower with copper-roofed crown, neighbouring blocks with classical cornices, ornamented spandrels, and ranks of regular punched windows. Cream-painted refrigerator boxcars line the foreground siding. Riveted-steel billboard armature at left. Cool Kodachrome haze over distant masonry, raking sunlight on western facades, blue-grey sky.
